All of the below are based on, or taken directly from, described entries in Xanathar's Guide to Everything starting on page 79.

The following details additional uses for the various tools and kits described in the PHB. Each description is by no means an exhaustive list and is more to just show the various things that kits can be used for. Other uses should be discussed with the DM.

Advantage. Proficiency in both a tool and skill that are valid for a particular check grants advantage on the check.

Added Benefit. A success in which a character has proficiency in both a tool and skill that are valid for the check can also gain additional information beyond being only proficient in one.

Alchemist's Supplies

Alchemist’s supplies enable a character to produce useful concoctions, such as acid or alchemist’s fire.

Components
  • Two glass beakers
  • A metal frame to hold a beaker
  • A glass stirring rod
  • A small mortar and pestle
  • A pouch of ingredients including: salt, powdered iron, purified water
Skills
  • Arcana: Additional information on arcana checks involving potions and similar materials
  • Investigation: Additional information on chemicals or substances used on an area
Special Uses
  • Alchemical Crafting: A character can buy raw materials which weigh 1lb per 50gp spent. As part of a long rest, you can make one dose of acid, alchemist’s fire, antitoxic, oil, perfume, soap, or other items left to the discretion of the DM. Subtract half the value of the created item from the total gp worth of raw material you are carrying.
  • Other Activities: Consult the Activity Table. Each activity takes 1 action.
Activity Table
Activity DC
Create a puff of thick smoke 10
Identify a poison 10
Identify a substance 15
Start a fire 15
Neutralize Acid 20
Brewer's Supplies

Brewing is the art of producing beer. Not only does beer serve as an alcoholic beverage, but the process of brewing purifies water. Crafting beer takes weeks of fermentation, but only a few hours of work.

Components
  • Large glass jug
  • A quantity of hops
  • A siphon
  • Several feet of tubing
Skills
  • History: Additional insight on History checks concerning events that involve alcohol as a specific element
  • Medicine: Additional insight when treating those suffering from alcohol poisoning or when using alcohol to dull pain
  • Persuasion: Can help ply someone with a drink
Special Uses
  • Purify Water: As part of a long rest, you can purify up to 6 gallons of water, or 1 gallon as part of a short rest.
  • Other Activities: Consult the Activity Table. Each activity takes 1 action where applicable.
Activity Table
Activity DC
Detect poison or impurities in a drink 10
Identify Alcohol 15
Ignore effects of alcohol 20
Calligrapher’s Supplies

Calligraphy treats writing as a delicate, beautiful art.Calligraphers produce text that is please to the eye, using a style that is difficult to forge. Their supplies also given them some ability to examine scripts and determine if they are legitimate, since a calligrapher’s training involves long hours of studying writing and attempting to replicate its style and design.

Components
  • Ink
  • A dozen sheets of parchment
  • 3 quills
Skills
  • Arcana: Aid in identifying who wrote scripts of magical nature
  • History: Augments benefit of successful checks made to analyze ancient writing, scrolls, text, runes, or frescoes
Special Uses
  • Decipher Treasure Map: Can make an INT check to determine a map’s age, whether a map includes any hidden messages or similar facts.
  • Other Activities: Consult the Activity Table. Each activity takes 1 hour.
Activity Table
Activity DC
Identify writer of nonmagical script 10
Determine writer’s state of mind 15
Spot forged text 15
Forge a Signature 20

Cartographer's Tools

Using cartographer’s tools, you can create accurate maps to make travel easier for yourself and those who come after you. These maps can range from large-scale depictions of mountain ranges to diagrams that show the layout of a dungeon level.

Components
  • A quill

  • Parchment
  • A part of compasses
  • Calipers
  • A ruler
Skills
  • Arcana, History, Religion, Nature: Additional information when used in conjunction with maps or geography based subjects
  • Survival: Predict patterns of various geological features, giving a better sense of direction
Special Uses
  • Craft a Map: While traveling, you can draw a map as you in addition to engaging in other activties
  • Other Activities: Consult the Activity Table
Activity Table

Estimate direction and distance to a landmark 15

Cobbler's Tools

Although the cobbler’s trade might seem too humble for an adventurer, a good pair of boots will see a character across a rugged wilderness and through deadly dungeons.

Components
  • Hammer
  • Awl
  • Knife
  • Shoe Stand
  • Cutter
  • Spare Leather
  • Thread
Skills
  • Arcana, History: Aid in identifying properties of magic boots as well as their history
  • Investigation: Can examine where someone has been or what they have encountered based on shoes
Special Uses
  • Maintain Shoes: As a part of a long rest, repair up to 6 characters shoes. For the next 24 hours, these characters may travel up to 10 hours a day without making saving throws for exhaustion.
  • Craft Hidden Compartment: With 8 hours of work, add a hidden compartment to a shoe. Can hold an object up to 3 inches long and 1 inch wide and deep. An INT check using this tool provides the DC needed to find the compartment.
  • Other Activities: Consult the Activity Table
Activity Table
Activity DC
Determine a shoe’s age and origin 10
Find a hidden compartment in a boot heel 15

Disguise Kit

The perfect tool for anyone who wants to engage in trickery, a disguise kit enables its owner to adopt a false identity.

Components
  • Cosmetics
  • Hair Dye
  • Small Props
  • A few pieces of clothing
Skills
  • Deception: Improved ability to weave convincing lies
  • Intimidation: Improved ability to intimidate while wearing a fearsome disguise
  • Performance: Improved acting if wearing an appropriate disguise
  • Persuasion: Improved ability to persuade if wearing an “official” looking disguise
Special Uses
  • Create Disguise: As part of a long rest, can create a disguise. The disguise takes 1 minute to don. Can only carry 1 disguise at a time, unless you have a bag of holding, or other similar method. A disguise weighs 1lb. Can also take 10 minutes to make moderate changes to appearance, or 30 minutes for extensive changes.
  • Other Activities: Consult the Activity Table
Activity Table
Activity DC
Cover inquiries or distinguishing marks 10

Forgery Kit

A forgery kit is designed to duplicate documents and to make it easier to copy a person’s seal or signature.

Components
  • Several different ink types
  • A variety of parchments and papers
  • Several quills
  • Seals and sealing wax
  • Gold and silver leaf
  • Small tools to sculpt wax to mimic a seal
Skills
  • Arcana: Can determine if a magic item is real or fake
  • Deception: A well forged document can lend credence to a lie
  • History: Improved ability to create fake historical documents or tell if an old document is authentic
  • Investigation: Improved ability in determining how an object was made and if it is genuine
  • Other Tools: Knowledge with other tools makes forgeries that much more believable
Special Uses
  • Quick Fake: As part of a short rest, can forge a document no longer than 1 page. For a long rest, no longer than 4 pages. An INT check determines the DC needed to spot the fake.
  • Other Activities: Consult the Activity Table
Activity Table
Activity DC
Mimic handwriting 15
Duplicate a wax seal 20
Gaming Set

Proficiency with a gaming set applies to one type of game, such as Three-Dragon Ante or games of chance that use dice.

Components
All the pieces needed to play a particular game.
Skills
  • History: Have knowledge of the history of the game such as important events or prominent figures associated with it
  • Insight: Improved ability of discerning lies, truths, and mood
  • Sleight of Hand: Improved ability at cheating in games, such as altering die, palming cards, or swapping pieces. Also, can more easily distract with dexterous movements.
Activity Table
Activity DC
Catch a player cheating 15
Gain insight into an opponent’s personality 15

Vehicles (Land, Water, Air)

Proficiency with vehicles grants the knowledge needed to handle vehicles of that type, along with knowledge of how to repair and maintain them. In addition, a character proficient with water vehicles is knowledgeable about anything a professional sailor would be familiar with, such as information about the sea and islands, tying knots, and assessing weather and sea conditions.

Skills
  • Arcana: Aid in uncovering lore of or determining the mechanisms of a magic vehicle
  • Investigation, Perception: Additional information when examining vehicles
Special Uses
  • Vehicle Handling: When piloting a vehicle, can apply proficiency bonus to the vehicle’s AC and saving throws
Activity Table
Activity DC
Navigate through rough terrain or waters 10
Assess a vehicle’s condition 15
Take a tight corner at high speeds 20

Thieves' Tools

Perhaps the most common tools used by adventurers, thieves’ tools are designed for picking locks and foiling traps. Proficiency with the tools also grants you a general knowledge of traps and locks.

Components
  • Small file
  • Set of lock picks
  • Small mirror mounted on a metal handle
  • Narrow-bladed scissors
  • Pliers
Skills
  • History: Greater recall on information of areas/locations renowned for traps
  • Investigation, Perception: Additional insight when looking for traps
Special Uses
  • Set a Trap: As part of a short rest, can create a trap with materials on hand. The check becomes the DC for someone to notice or disable the trap. Damage is based off of materials used, such as damage of weapon or poison, or ½ the total check, as deemed appropriate by the DM.
Activity Table
Activity DC
Pick a lock Varies
Disable a trap Varies
Tinker's Tools

A set of tinker’s tools is designed to enable you to repair many mundane objects. Though you can’t manufacture much with tinker’s tools, you can mend torn clothes, sharpen a worn sword, and patch a tattered suit of chain mail.

Components
  • Variety of hand tools
  • Thread
  • Needles
  • Whetstone
  • Scraps of cloth
  • Leather
  • Small pot of glue
Skills
  • History: Determine age and origin of an object, even if it’s only a partial.
  • Investigation: Gain knowledge of how an object was damaged and how long ago.
Special Uses
  • Can restore 10 hit points to a damaged object per hour of work. Requires the raw materials needed to repair. For metal objects, also required a flame hot enough to shape metal.
Activity Table
Activity DC
Temporarily repair a disabled device 10
Repair an item in half the time 15
Improvise a temporary item using scraps 20
