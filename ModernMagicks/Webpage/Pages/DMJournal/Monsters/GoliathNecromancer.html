Spellcasting
The Goliath Necromancer is a 9th-level spellcaster. Its spellcasting ability is Intelligence (spell save DC 14, +6 to hit with spell attacks). The Goliath Necromancer has the following spells prepared:
  1. Cantrips: True Strike, Chill Touch, Ray of Frost
  2. 1st level (4 slots): Shield, False Life, Inflict Wounds
  3. 2nd level (3 slots): Ray of Enfeeblement, Gentle Repose
  4. 3rd level (3 slots): Sleet Storm, Animate Dead, Vampiric Touch
  5. 4th level (3 slots): Ice Storm, Blight
  6. 5th level (1 slots): Contagion

The Goliath Necromancer makes 2 longsword attacks. It can use it's Life Drain in place of one longsword attack.
Life Drain

Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5ft., one creature. Hit: 5 (1d6+2) necrotic damage. The target must succeed on a DC 13 Consititution saving thow or its hit point maximum is reduced by an amount equal to the damage taken. This reduction lasts until the target finishes a long rest. The target dies if this effect educes its hit point maximum to 0.

A humanoid slain by this attack rises 24 hours later as a zombie under the wight's control, unless the humanois is restored to life or its body is destroyed. The Goliath Necromancer can have no more than twelve undead under its control at one time
