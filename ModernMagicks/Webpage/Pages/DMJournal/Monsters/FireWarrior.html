Heated Body
Any creature that touches or makes an attack against the Flame Warrior takes 7 (2d6) fire damage. Only active after using Flame Aura, for the duration specified.

Actions

Multiattack
The Fire Warrior makes 2 Longsword attacks.
Longsword
Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5ft., one creature. Hit: 22 (2d8+14) slashing damage.
Combustion Touch (Recharge 5-6)
The Fire Warrior can ignite a target when making a longsword or unarmed attack. The target must make a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw or catch fire, taking an additional 5 (1d10) fire damage at the beginning of its next turn. Until someone takes an action to douse the fire, the creature takes 5 (1d10) fire damage at the start of each of its turns
Flame Aura
As an action, the Flame Warrior may heat it’s own armor, gaining it’s Heated Body effect for 2 turns. This action also spawns 1 Firenewt Warlock from his body, landing in a random location within 10 ft.
