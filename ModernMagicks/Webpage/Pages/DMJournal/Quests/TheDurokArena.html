The Durok Arena

The winter season is a month long divided into 4 week leagues. The final 5th week is the championship.

Leagues

Solo League
Face off against other contenders to become the arena champion.
Team League
Face off against various difficulties of monsters for money and glory. Each difficulty type is once per day.

Rewards

Solo League
50g per win
Team League
10g for easy
20g for medium
100g for hard
500g for deadly
