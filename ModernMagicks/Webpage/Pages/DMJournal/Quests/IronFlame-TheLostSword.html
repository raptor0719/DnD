Iron Flame: The Lost Sword

Description

Cabral has asked the adventurers to go to a tomb to the north, in the Deadlands, to retrieve a sword from the body of one of the founding members of the Iron Flame. He says the tomb is nestled in a rocky outcropping on the border of the autumn-lands and the Deadlands.
Upon descending the stairs and entering a tomb there will be a scroll in a leather bound. On the scroll will be a letter from Lord Thargas saying the following
  • He expected the iron flame to come eventually
  • He wishes to test the current members
  • Trials will be based on the founding tenets of the guild

Trials

Trial of Honor
"Rules and laws must be obeyed, stand on these rules and face your trials"
There will be 4 buttons in a large room, each with a restriction
  1. A panel with a fist on it, fight a Scarecrow pg 268
  2. A panel with a bow on it, fights a Dire Wolf pg 321
  3. A panel with a dagger on it, fights a Harpy pg 181
  4. A panel with a rope on it, fights a Tiger pg 339
Trial of Trust
"One will be chosen, to do nothing not fight, he must trust in his friends, to do what is right"
A large room with a 6 pillars around in 2 rows of 3. There are 9 foot walls connecting the 3 pillars in each row.
In the center of the room is a pedestal with a gem on it. Upon being picked up the gem will be absorbed into the carrier.
A manticore (pg 213) will appear and chase the gem wielder. It will only attack others to get them out of the way.
Trial of Bravery
"Fight and die with courage in your hearts"
A small hallways with a fogged door ar the end. Once entered they are in a windy and stormy mountain peak. After exploring, a Storm Giant (pg 156) appears and they must fight it.
After defeating, or dying, to the giant, the players wake up to the same door, but with fog no longer emenating.
This would conclude the trials and inside the room is where the Glaive of Thargas WOULD reside. Instead, there is simply a large X carved into the pedastal. Upon further investigation, a small tunnel has been dug through from the surface.

Rewards
