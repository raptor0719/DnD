Bugbear Silver Mine

Description

A contigent of bugbears from the mountains to the east has set up here to dig for precious metals. Their primary find has been silver. Working the mine are a group of bugbears with a leader, as well as a couple of ogres.
In 5 days of discovering this quest, a group of 5 bugbears w/ morningstars will come with a cart of 30 days of rations.
