The Werebear

Description

Ichi Kastan is going to investigate why the town of Autumn's Edge has ceased contact. He was going with the Iron Flame to investigate rumors of a passage into the Underdark in the northern mountains. Another ranger left to check on the town, but has not made contact since.
The party will come across the town and find dried blood and eaten corpses scattered across the scape. The adventurers will be able to track a trail of blood that goes away from the town, into a small cave in the side of a large hill.
Inside they will find the corpse of the missing ranger, and two remaining villagers (named Joshua and Larq). They say the ranger brought them here for safety, but died just a day ago. They've have been hiding here for awhile. One other villager, Vincent, was not there the night the town was attacked, and they believe he is still out there.
Vincent is the creature that attacked the town, and is a werebear. Vincent was the town's huntsman and protector. The adventurers will need to acquire silver weapons to kill him. Or they could attempt to find a healer to lift the curse.
